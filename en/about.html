About Reborn Transport

Our Story

How we became Batam - Bintan's most trusted transport service provider

Our Driving Mission

Reborn Transport was established with a simple mission: to provide reliable, professional transportation services to residents and visitors of Batam - Bintan.

What started as a small operation with just three vehicles has grown into one of the region's most trusted transport providers, serving thousands of customers annually.

Our commitment to quality service, professional drivers, and well-maintained vehicles has remained unchanged since day one.

Today - Your Trusted Partner

We now operate a modern fleet of over 30 vehicles, from compact cars to large buses, serving both local residents and international visitors.

Our team of 25+ professional drivers is trained to the highest standards, with many fluent in English to better serve our international clientele.

As a locally-owned business, we understand the unique transportation needs of Batam - Bintan, making us the preferred choice for both short trips and long-term contracts.
